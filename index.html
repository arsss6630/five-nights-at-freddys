NIGHT SHIFT NOTICE

You are the new night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your job is simple: watch the security cameras and make sure nothing goes wrong. But be warned - the animatronic characters tend to... wander at night. Use your limited power wisely. Can you survive until 6 AM?

About FNAF

Five Nights at Freddy's is the iconic indie horror game created by Scott Cawthon that launched a massive franchise. Released in 2014, it has since spawned numerous sequels, spin-offs, novels, and even a major motion picture in 2023 with a sequel coming in 2025.

In this point-and-click survival horror game, you must monitor security cameras, conserve power, and close doors at the right moment to survive attacks from possessed animatronic characters. Each night gets progressively harder as the animatronics become more aggressive.

Survival Tips

  • 01 Check cameras frequently but briefly - they drain power fast
  • 02 Listen for footsteps and breathing sounds near the doors
  • 03 Only close doors when absolutely necessary
  • 04 Watch Pirate Cove - Foxy gets angry if ignored
  • 05 Conserve power for the final hours
